Email export guide

Start by downloading your mailbox. We support .mbox and .pst files from Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, Apple Mail, Thunderbird, Proton Mail, Zoho, iCloud, and more.

Follow the full walkthrough for your provider on the dedicated email export guide, then return here to upload the archive.

Tip: If your archive is larger than 20 GB, split the export into multiple files or run multiple exports so each upload stays within the limit.

Troubleshooting

  • Upload stuck at 0%? Refresh the page and try again. Very large files may take a few moments before the progress bar updates.
  • “File too large” error? The archive exceeded the 20 GB limit. Split the export and upload each part separately.
  • “Not ready” when downloading? The job is still parsing. Wait until the status shows done before retrying.
  • Job failed? Copy the job ID from the upload URL and include it when contacting support so we can investigate.
Still stuck? Email support@mbox-csv.com with the job ID and a description of what happened.

Contact

The fastest way to get help is to email support@mbox-csv.com. Include:

  • Your job ID (if you started an upload).
  • The approximate size of the archive.
  • Any error messages you saw.

You can also use the contact form if you prefer not to email directly.
